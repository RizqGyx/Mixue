About Us

Mixue is a passionate purveyor of delightful ice cream and tea experiences. Born from a shared love for these sweet treats, Mixue's journey is one of creativity and dedication. We take pride in offering high-quality products, certified Halal by LPPOM MUI, crafted from the finest ingredients. Our diverse menu caters to all tastes, from classic flavors to innovative creations. Beyond our offerings, Mixue fosters a vibrant community, always exploring new flavor combinations, and ensuring a welcoming environment for our customers. Join us to savor the sweet symphony of love at Mixue, where every scoop and sip is a celebration of life's sweet moments.
